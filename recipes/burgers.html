Best Hamburger Ever

Description

Get ready to be the star of the neighbourhood barbecue with the best burger ever! This burger is jam packed with flavours that will make you hungrier and craving for more after every bite!

Ingredients

  • 11⁄2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1⁄2 onion, finely chopped
  • 1⁄2 cup shredded Colby Jack or Cheddar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 ounce envelope dry onion soup mix
  • 1 clove garlice, minced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon crushed dried rosemary
  • salt and pepper to taste
